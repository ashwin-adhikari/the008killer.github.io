CTF

Capture the Flag (CTF) in computer security is an exercise in which participants attempt to find text strings, called "flags", which are secretly hidden in purposefully-vulnerable programs or websites.

HackTheBox

Hack The Box (HTB) is an online platform that provides hands-on cybersecurity training through real-world penetration testing challenges. It offers a variety of machines and challenges for users to test and improve their ethical hacking skills.
